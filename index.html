Scope
In the US alone, 10 billion animals are raised in inhumane conditions and slaughtered every year.
We believe that solutions will come from efforts in advocacy and policy paired with advances in culinary research.

Ultimately, changes in consumer behavior will require viable plant‑based alternatives that surpass conventional options in both affordability and flavor.
